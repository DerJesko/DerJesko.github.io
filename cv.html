Research Interests

My research interests are broadly in theoretical cryptography. Though, I do not shy away from practical work. So far my publications have mostly stuck to communication efficient delegation of computation and restricting cryptographic adversaries in unusual ways. I am currently expanding into proof systems and obfuscation.
